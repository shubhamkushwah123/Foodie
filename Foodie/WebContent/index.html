About Us

Far away from home, Do you miss the taste of homemade Indian food, if yes, then FoodiePoint is the place for you, where you can feel the aroma and enjoy the blended taste of Indian spices. We guarantee fresh food and our meals are very carefully prepared to please your taste buds. We ensure high-quality ingredients and on time delivery of orders. If you are planning a Farewell party, Birthday Party, get together or any event, we are ready to serve you with our mouthwatering recipes. We only accept advance orders for more than €50.
